Built for simplicity

Everything you need to download videos, nothing you don't.

Paste and Go

Simply paste a URL and start downloading. Preview the video before you download with title, thumbnail, and format info.

Self-Contained

yt-dlp and ffmpeg are bundled inside the app. No additional software to install or configure.

1000+ Sites

YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, and hundreds more. If yt-dlp supports it, so does Downlink.

Playlist Support

Download entire playlists with one click. Each video is added to the queue and downloaded automatically.

SponsorBlock

Automatically skip or remove sponsor segments, intros, and outros from your downloaded videos.

Subtitles

Download and embed subtitles in multiple languages. Auto-generated captions are also supported.
